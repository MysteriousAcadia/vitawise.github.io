Vitawise Simulate

A "flight-simulator" type environment for surgeons that allows you to work on the 3D renderings of the body(organs) of a particular patient that are constructed from various scans input into the system and patient medical history. This will allow them to be familiar with the various complications that may arise in the surgical procedure.

Vitawise Alfred

The future of long-distance surgery, Alfred is an AI agent that tracks your trial procedures. It runs this video feed through an AI engine to analyse the procedure. It uses this analysis to understand the surgical procedure and be present in the surgical theatre as a guide for the surgeon, alerting relevant people in case of any deviation from the original procedure.

Vitawise Hub

Our mixed reality environment allows you to collaborate by bringing in surgeons from anywhere around the world to supervise your trial/real surgeries, provide real-time second opinions, suggest alternative techniques for handling complications and much more. This extends the surgical theatre to beyond the premises of your hospital/medical institution.
